PHOTO VIA @MakerereU Kampala, Uganda | THE INDEPENDENT | Reports from Makerere University indicate that Police Fire Brigade is trying to put out a fire which has now covered almost half of the roof of the institution’s main building. All indications are the fire started just after mid-night. “The fire brigade is on ground. Everyone is trying their best. The fire is heavy and sprouting from the right side of the Building. The fire flames are heavy coming through right side of the roof which has sunk in. We all need to pray for the Ivory Tower,” tweeted @MakerereU at about 1am, Sunday. Soon after the initial reports came out, fire spread and covered all floors of the right side of the building. That entire wing looks likely to cave in. The main building houses several offices including the university’s main-hall. The printery and Vice Chancellor’s office are also located at the same building.
